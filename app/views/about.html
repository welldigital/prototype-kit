About us

Who are we

Well Pharmacy is the largest independent pharmacy chain in the UK, and the third largest pharmacy overall.

How we can help

We have 780 stores nationwide with friendly, expert healthcare professionals to help you make the most of your health. For example, repeat prescriptions, stop smoking advice, medicines advice.

Our history

Previously the Co-operative Pharmacy, we were bought by the Bestway Group in 2014, and rebranded as Well.

Our corporate social responsibility

We support the national network of WEEE (waste electrical and electronic equipment) recycling centres, so you can recycle your electrical and electronic devices for free. Find a recycle point.

We want to reduce the use of single-use plastic carrier bags and the litter they cause so we usually charge 5p a bag.

We're committed to making sure modern slavery is not taking place in our business or supply chain. Read our modern slavery statement.

Official charity partner

We’re working with the Stroke Association to raise awareness on the link between high blood pressure and stroke in local communities.

Our aim is to raise £250,000 and we’re doing a whole host of fundraising activity to try to reach our goals.
