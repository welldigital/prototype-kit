Prescriptions Collection Service

Each Well branch collects prescriptions from designated local doctors' surgeries. Please check with your pharmacist to find out if your doctor's surgery is within our prescription collection range.

The pharmacist must be in possession of your prescription in order to be able to dispense it. If, for any reason, your doctor has not released your prescription to us we cannot dispense it. Should this occur, please contact your doctor for more details and to get your repeat service up and running again with us as soon as possible.
