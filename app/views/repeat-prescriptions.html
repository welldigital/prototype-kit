Repeat prescriptions

Getting repeat prescriptions from your Well Pharmacy

With repeat prescriptions you don’t need to pick up your prescription from your GP every time it’s renewed. Well Pharmacy will take care of everything.

All you have to do is drop by your local Well Pharmacy and pick up your medicines. Someone else like a carer can pick them up for you too.

How to set up repeat prescriptions

  • 1

    Ask your GP to send your prescription to your nearest Well Pharmacy - or take it yourself.

  • 2

    Go to the pharmacy and tell them you want to set up repeat prescriptions - they’ll organise everything with your GP.

How repeat prescriptions work

Approximately 7 days before your medicines are due to run out the pharmacy will contact you to confirm when your repeat prescription will be ready. You can pick it up yourself or get free home delivery.

Managing your repeat prescription

You change GP

Let your new GP know which Well Pharmacy you’re using for your prescriptions.

You move house

Let your GP and Well pharmacy know you’ve moved.

Your GP changes your prescription

You don’t have to do anything.

Your Well Pharmacy will get the new prescription from your GP and have your medicines ready for you when you need them.

You no longer need your prescription

Tell your Well Pharmacy.

You can do this by phone or drop into the branch.

Problems or worries with your medicines

If you have any problems taking your medicines or any other worries, just drop into your local Well Pharmacy and talk to your pharmacist.

Your Well Pharmacist can, for example, help with:

  • alternative to pills - if you find them hard to swallow
  • side effects of a new medicine and what to do about them
  • confusion about which medicine to take when

Where necessary, your Well Pharmacist will talk to your GP to sort out a problem. You usually don’t need to go back and see your doctor.
