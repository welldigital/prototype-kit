Repeat Prescriptions

With repeat prescriptions you don’t have to pick up your prescription slip from your GP every time it’s renewed. Well Pharmacy will take care of everything.

All you have to do is drop by your Well Pharmacy and pick up your new medicines - we will have them all ready for you.

How to set up repeat prescriptions

  • 1

    Drop your first paper prescription off at a Well Pharmacy near you or have your GP send it electronically.

  • 2

    Tell the pharmacist that you would like to set up repeat prescriptions with their branch.

  • 3

    All future prescriptions will now be sent to this pharmacy.

  • 4

    A week before you run out of your medication go to your Well Pharmacy and pick up your new batch of medicines. If you get your medicines delivered we’ll come by your house and drop them off on the usual day.

You have to pay for your medicines every time you pick up a new batch unless you’re exempt.
