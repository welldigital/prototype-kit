Having medicines delivered to your house

Well Pharmacy can deliver your prescriptions for free to your house.

Who can get free delivery

We offer this service for people who are housebound and on repeat prescriptions.

How to set up free delivery

Phone your local Well Pharmacy and ask them to set up free delivery for you.

Our pharmacy staff will talk you through when your deliveries will start and what day of the week you’ll usually get your prescriptions.

Making changes to your delivery

Phone your local Well Pharmacy if you need to make any changes to your delivery. For example, you might want to change the day of the week the driver is coming by.

If you move house and want your medicines delivered to a new address, it’s helpful if you have your NHS number ready when you phone you phone your local Well Pharmacy.

Our delivery team

All our drivers are trusted Well employees. You will usually see the same driver around the same time every week.

Our drivers are friendly and approachable. If you have any concerns about your delivery, you can talk to them about it. They’re also usually up for a friendly chat.
