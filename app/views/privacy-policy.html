Privacy Policy

Plain English Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and we will always:

  • keep your information safe and secure
  • be clear about how we collect your information and why

How we collect your information

We collect your personal information when you

  • shop with us
  • use our services (repeat prescriptions)
  • use our social media channels

Our pharmacists may also ask to access your NHS records for medication and allergy history [when they’re doing your prescription].

Why we collect your information

We use your personal information for things like:

  • confirming your identification when you contact us
  • providing you with the best level of customer support.
  • contacting you about your order such as a prescription or product recalls
  • for prevention of crime and fraud

Sharing your information

We may share your information with third parties where required or permitted by law. In such cases we will do so legally and with due regard to your privacy.

Your rights

You can:

  • get a copy of the information we hold about you
  • change information that is incorrect
  • opt out of promotions and offers

To do any of this, contact our customer care team.

For copies of your information we’ll ask for your name, address, date of birth and a copy of a photo ID (for example, a passport or driving licence). It can take up to 40 days to send you the copies.
